Projects

Frozen Dessert from Hemp Milk

This project focused on developing a dairy-free frozen dessert using hemp milk, targeted at vegan and lactose-intolerant consumers. The work involved optimizing formulations to balance texture, taste, and stability while maintaining nutritional quality. Trials included emulsifier and stabilizer selection to achieve creaminess comparable to traditional ice cream. This research showcased the potential of hemp milk as a sustainable and nutrient-rich base, offering consumers a healthier and inclusive alternative in the frozen dessert market.
